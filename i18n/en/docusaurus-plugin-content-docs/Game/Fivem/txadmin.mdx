# Configuring TXAdmin on Hebergtonserv.com

Follow these steps to configure TXAdmin after ordering a server on `hebergtonserv.com`.

## Accessing the Panel and Initial Setup

1. Log in to your server from your preferred panel on `hebergtonserv.com`.

2. Ensure that the TXAdmin option is properly set in **Configuration -> Startup Settings**.

   ![Startup Settings](https://i.imgur.com/cUZs0bw.png)

3. Activate the TXAdmin option.

   ![Activate TXAdmin](https://i.imgur.com/zfnh5c7.png)

## Starting the Server and Configuring TXAdmin

4. Once activated, go to the console and start your server.

   ![Starting the Server](https://i.imgur.com/oVWbPvu.png)
   ![Server Console](https://i.imgur.com/QPzdwnt.png)

5. Once started, a link will be displayed in the console with a PIN code.

   ![TXAdmin Link](https://i.imgur.com/KKch3fi.png)

6. Follow the link, enter the PIN, and click on "Link Account".

   ![Link Account](https://i.imgur.com/i3OQDgR.png)

7. Authorize TXAdmin to access your CFX account.

   ![Authorize CFX](https://i.imgur.com/Y1wRJuA.png)

8. Create your password, check the TOS (Terms of Service), and then click on "Create Master Account".

   ![Create Account](https://i.imgur.com/mWQaPs1.png)

## Final Configuration of TXAdmin

9. The next steps involve configuring TXAdmin, which will be linked to the root of your Hebergtonserv server. Click "Next".

   ![Next Steps](https://i.imgur.com/H4yR2AH.png)

10. Enter the name of your server and click "Next".

    ![Server Name](https://i.imgur.com/MmtaHft.png)

11. For "Deployment Type", select "Existing Server Data". (We will cover an autopush version in another tutorial.)

    ![Deployment Type](https://i.imgur.com/JOsdFZl.png)

12. Enter the physical path where your `server.cfg` is located (`/home/container/`).

    ![Server Path](https://i.imgur.com/MdcTdxk.png)

13. TXAdmin will auto-detect your `server.cfg`. Click "Save".

    ![Auto-detection of server.cfg](https://i.imgur.com/kYrNheS.png)

14. All that's left is to press the "Save & Start Server" button.

    ![Start Server](https://i.imgur.com/75xMGaS.png)

By following these instructions, you will have configured TXAdmin for your FiveM server hosted on Hebergtonserv. This setup will allow you to manage your server more effectively and access advanced administrative tools.
